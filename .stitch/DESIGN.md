# Design System: Nudge AI Immersive Workspace

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
A cinematic, 3D immersive "future workspace" experience in LIGHT MODE. The visual style is built around light, airy glassmorphism, soft shadows, translucent layers, floating depth, and realistic lighting. The mood is calm, intelligent, motivating, and playful. Backgrounds are soft ivory and warm off-white.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Canvas Ivory** (#FAF6F0) — Warm, soft, high-luxury off-white background
- **Pure Surface** (rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.75)) — Translucent glassmorphic card base
- **Headline Indigo** (#1E1B4B) — Deep indigo/charcoal for bold, clean text
- **Secondary Body** (#4B5563) — Muted gray-zinc for body text
- **Accent Electric Blue** (#3B82F6) — Primary active cues and CTA fills
- **Urgency Coral** (#FB923C) — Soft warning accents for deadline countdowns
- **Critical Red-Pink** (#FB7185) — High-urgency deadlines, alerts, and priority dots
- **Mint Success** (#2DD4BF) — Fresh completed states, checkmarks, and plant growth metrics
- **Interactive Gradients** — Subtle pale lavender, peach, and light blue backdrops

## 3. Typography Rules
- **Display/Headlines**: Satoshi / Plus Jakarta Sans — Extra bold, tight tracking (-0.03em), large line height
- **Body**: Satoshi / Plus Jakarta Sans — Soft leading, max 60ch readability width
- **Mono**: Geist Mono — Status chips, priority ranks, focus timer values

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons**: 3D rounded pills. Expand (+3% scale) and drop-shadow glow on hover; depress (-1px) on click.
* **Cards**: Layered glass panel borders (1px white/30%) with soft tinted drop shadows corresponding to their priority state (e.g. coral card casts soft coral shadow).
* **AI Orb Widget**: Animated concentric pulsing spheres representing Nudge AI.

## 5. Motion & Interaction
- Smooth scroll, custom ring follow cursor trail, magnetic buttons.
- Slowly morphing 3D background grid/mesh representing time awareness.
- Staggered waterfall reveals on mount.
